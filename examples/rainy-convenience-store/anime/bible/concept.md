# Concept

## Title

雨夜耳机失踪案

## Viewer Promise

In the first three seconds, viewers see a water-fearing cat-eared detective flinch from rain inside a midnight convenience store while a missing talking earbud whispers her name from the freezer aisle.

## Logline

A cat-eared detective who is afraid of water must recover a talking missing earbud on a stormy night, only to learn the earbud's evidence points to her own disappearance.

## Audience

Viewers who like short-form anime mystery, cute-but-tense character reactions, rain-night suspense, and a final twist that invites comments.

## Target Format

- Platform: vertical short video.
- Aspect ratio: 9:16.
- Runtime: 60 seconds.
- Language: zh-CN dialogue and short zh-CN subtitles.
- Model depth: offline provider-neutral specs only.

## Character Anchors

- Cat-eared detective: yellow raincoat, blue scarf, small magnifier, black bob haircut, expressive ears, anxious-but-brave voice.
- Talking earbud: tiny white earbud with cyan waveform glow, intimate electronic whisper, no brand marks.
- Rain as antagonist: heavy rain, puddles, glass-door reflections, dripping ceiling leak, water framed as a psychological obstacle.

## Visual Anchors

- Rainy convenience store at midnight, neon reflections, narrow aisles, freezer glow.
- Clean anime line art, expressive acting, readable vertical composition.
- Suspense comedy tone: cute facial acting against tense lighting and sound.
- Color anchors: yellow raincoat, blue scarf, cyan waveform, magenta-blue neon, cold freezer white.
