# Concept

## Viewer Promise

In the first three seconds, viewers see a cat-eared detective flinch from rain inside a convenience store while a missing earbud whispers her name from the freezer aisle.

## Audience

Viewers who like short-form anime mystery, cute-but-tense character reactions, and a final twist that invites comments.

## Visual Anchors

- Cat-eared detective, yellow raincoat, blue scarf, small magnifier.
- Rainy convenience store at midnight, neon reflections, narrow aisles, freezer glow.
- Clean anime line art, expressive acting, readable vertical composition.

## Non-goals

- No real provider call.
- No final public video.
- No copyrighted character reference.
